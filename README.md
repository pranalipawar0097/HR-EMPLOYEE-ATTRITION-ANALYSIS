# HR Employee Attrition Analysis | SQL

##  Project Overview
This project analyzes employee attrition data from IBM HR Analytics to uncover the key factors influencing employee turnover. The analysis focuses on departments, job roles, income levels, overtime, experience, and job satisfaction using SQL-based queries.

##  Objectives
- Calculate the overall employee attrition rate  
- Identify departments and job roles with the highest attrition  
- Analyze the impact of monthly income on attrition  
- Examine the relationship between overtime and attrition  
- Study how experience and job satisfaction affect employee retention  

##  Tools & Skills Used
- SQL (SELECT, WHERE, GROUP BY, HAVING, CASE, JOINS)
- Aggregate functions for business-driven insights
- Analytical thinking and problem-solving

##  Key Insights
- Certain departments and job roles show significantly higher attrition  
- Employees working overtime have a higher likelihood of leaving  
- Lower income and low job satisfaction are strong contributors to attrition  
- Experience level plays a critical role in employee retention  

##  Dataset
- IBM HR Analytics Employee Attrition Dataset

##  Outcome
The analysis provides actionable insights that can help HR teams improve employee retention strategies and reduce attrition through data-driven decisions.
